  6. 分别以$X_i$, $Y_i(i=1,2,\ldots,20)$记为20台机器第一阶段和第二阶段的修理时间,
    以$Z=\sum_{i=1}^{20}(X_i+Y_i)$记为修理时间总和. 由题意可知$E(X_i)=0.2$, $D(X_i)=0.2^2$, $E(Y_i)=0.3$, $D(Y_i)=0.3^2$, 从而有
    $$\begin{gather*} E(X_i+Y_i)=E(X_i)+E(Y_i)=0.5,\\ D(X_i+Y_i)=D(X_i)+E(Y_i)=0.2^2+0.3^2,\end{gather*}$$

    以及
    $$\begin{aligned} P\{Z<8\}&=P\left\{\frac{Z-20\cdot 0.5}{\sqrt{20\cdot(0.2^2+0.3^2)}}<\frac{8-20\cdot 0.5}{\sqrt{20\cdot(0.2^2+0.3^2)}}\right\}\\ &=\varPhi(-1.24)=0.1075.\end{aligned}$$
